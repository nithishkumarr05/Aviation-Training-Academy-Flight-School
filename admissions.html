Eligibility

Admission Requirements

To ensure your success, we've established clear requirements for all applicants. Review the checklist below to confirm your eligibility.

Age

Minimum 17 years old for Private Pilot, 18 for Commercial.

Education

High school diploma or equivalent (GED).

FAA Medical Certificate

Class 1, 2, or 3 medical certificate depending on program.

English Proficiency

Demonstrated reading, writing, and speaking ability.

Background Check

FAA-required security background clearance.
