About SkyWings

Where Excellence Takes Flight

For over a decade, SkyWings Aviation Academy has been shaping the next generation of professional pilots with world-class training, state-of-the-art aircraft, and an unwavering commitment to safety.

Our Story Join Us
Our Heritage

A Legacy of Aviation Excellence

Founded in 2013 by a team of veteran airline captains, SkyWings Aviation Academy was born from a simple belief: that exceptional pilot training should be accessible, rigorous, and inspiring. What started as a single aircraft and a handful of students has grown into one of the most respected flight schools in the country.

Today, we operate a modern fleet of 15+ aircraft, have trained over 700 pilots, and maintain a 98% first-attempt pass rate on FAA checkrides. Our alumni fly for major airlines across the globe, and our reputation for excellence continues to soar.
